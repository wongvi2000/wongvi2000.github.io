Shop

We carry a large inventory of unique Oriental antiques and furniture for wholesale and retail. You will find exquisite one of a kind collectables such as snuff bottles with delicate artwork, jade art pieces, other jewelry from precious materials, wood & stone carvings, and porcelain & terracotta figures.

Our furniture items includes various wood & coromandel screens with art work, painting scrolls, porcelain vases, rosewood cabinets, shelves, tables, chairs, and stands. Visit us at our downtown San Francisco storefront to view our products, which are shipped worldwide.

About Us

We specialize in Chinese antiques and furniture.

The company established in 1974 has been in business & located in the historical San Francisco Union Square shopping district.
