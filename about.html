About

You will find exquisite collectibles from the Han, Ming and other Chinese dynasties, including terracotta statues, porcelian figures, snuff bottles and jade statues. There are also 19th century paintings from mainland China, Korea, and Japan.



History

Fonded in 1974 with our storefront location at 535 Sutter St. in heart of San Francisco Union Square, we continue to offer our clients the finest in Asian antiques and furniture.
